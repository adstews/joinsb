FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Sendblue send real iMessages?

▼
Sendblue uses a proprietary infrastructure that routes your messages through Apple's iMessage protocol. This means your messages arrive as blue bubbles on iPhones, not as green SMS texts. They skip A2P carrier registration entirely and show up in the native Messages app just like a text from a friend.

What happens if the recipient doesn't have an iPhone?

▼
Sendblue automatically detects whether a phone number is registered with iMessage. If the recipient is on Android or doesn't have iMessage enabled, the message is automatically routed via SMS as a fallback. You don't need to manage this - it's handled automatically per message.

Do I need A2P (10DLC) registration to use Sendblue?

▼
No. A2P/10DLC registration is required for business SMS messaging through carriers. Since Sendblue sends iMessages, not SMS, you don't need to go through the lengthy A2P registration process. This means you can start sending messages immediately without the weeks-long approval process that SMS platforms require.

Which CRMs does Sendblue integrate with?

▼
Sendblue integrates with all major CRMs including HubSpot, Salesforce, GoHighLevel, Zapier, Make (formerly Integromat), and any platform that supports webhooks or REST APIs. Setup typically takes less than 2 minutes - just plug in your API key and configure your webhook URL.

How are read receipts and typing indicators useful?

▼
Read receipts tell you exactly when a lead opens your message, so your team can time follow-up calls perfectly. Typing indicators let you know when someone is actively composing a reply, so reps can stay ready. These signals eliminate guesswork and let your team focus on leads who are actively engaged, rather than chasing people who never saw the message.
